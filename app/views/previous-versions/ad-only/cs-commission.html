Does your advert adhere to Civil Service Commission Recruitment Principles?

The Recruitment Principles explain the legal requirement that selection for appointment to the Civil Service must be on:
  • merit
  • the basis of fair and open competition

They also set out and describe the:
  • circumstances in which you can make appointments as exceptions
  • responsibilities of departments and agencies to meet this need
Departments and agencies are free to develop their own approaches to recruitment. As long as they are consistent with the Recruitment Principles.
