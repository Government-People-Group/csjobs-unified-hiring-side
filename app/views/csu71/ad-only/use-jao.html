Do you want to optimise your job advert?

If you select 'yes', we will run your job advert through the Job Advert Optimiser.

This is an AI tool that will compare your job advert with previous Civil Service job adverts. It will use these previous adverts to estimate how well yours will perform.

The results from the tool might include insights such as the:

  • number of applicants it expects you'd receive
  • specific candidate skills you will likely attract
  • expected quality of applicants
  • percentage of applicants who are female or from ethnic minorities

The tool will also provide suggestions on how to improve your job description. For instance, if it suggests that your advert may not attract enough applicants with the skills you want, it can suggest improvements to the content of your job advert.

Please note that the tool's results are only estimates and suggestions. There is a lot that influences the performance of a job advert beyond the advert text which also needs to be considered carefully - such has how it is promoted.

The tool will only make recommendations - you do not have to change any of your ad if you do not want to.

It can take up to a 30 seconds for your results to appear.
