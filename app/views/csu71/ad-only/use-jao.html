Do you want to optimise your job advert?

If you select 'yes', we will run your job advert through the Job Advert Optimiser.

This is an AI tool which will check your advert for issues such as:

  • gender-biased language
  • language that might discourage applicants with disabilities from applying

It can also make suggestions - both general and role-specific - that may improve your advert and lead to better candidate engagement. It does this by looking at job advert content from successful Civil Service campaigns in the past.

The optimiser will only make recommendations - you do not have to change any of your ad if you do not want to.

It can take up to a 30 seconds for your results to appear.
