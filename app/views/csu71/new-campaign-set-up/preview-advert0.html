Person specification

Essential criteria

For this role, you will be required to:

  • deal with wide ranging enquiries and follow through to resolution
  • have experience of working on their own initiative as-well as collaboratively as part of a team
  • quickly develop working relationships
  • develop solutions to address problems
  • work flexibly to meet ever changing priorities
  • manage a diverse workload
  • have excellent communications skills, both oral and written

Key responsibilities

For this role, you will have key responsibilities to carry out on a day-to-day basis, like:

  • provide administrative support to the team
  • ensure projects focus on outcomes and deliver on time
  • assisting in the development of proposals for funding including drafting up content, editing and checking accuracy
  • drafting and providing content for progress reports on projects including extracting and collating information
  • assisting the team in delivering key milestones and evidencing action logs
  • setting up and supporting key stakeholder meetings and track progress on agreed actions

Who you will work with

We are looking for an experienced and enthusiastic Administrative Officer to provide administrative support, initially to our National Schools’ Programme. Reporting to the National Schools’ Coordinator you will provide key administrative and organisational support to enable the effective running of the project.

What we offer

Benefits include:

  • Learning and development tailored to your role
  • An environment with flexible working options
  • A culture encouraging inclusion and diversity
  • A Civil Service pension with an average employer contribution of 27%
  • Entitlement to a minimum start of 23 days annual leave

How to apply

We'll assess you against these behaviours during the application process:

  • Managing a Quality Service
  • Delivering at Pace
  • Working Together
  • Communicating and Influencing

You should write your answers with these behaviours in mind. For more help with our behaviours, you can see our success profiles.

Find out about our selection process.

Who can apply

We welcome applications from the following schemes:

  • Great Place to work for Veterans
  • Prison leaver recruitment
